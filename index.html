About Our Camp

istanova Summer Camp is your perfect destination for 6 days and 5 nights of fun and excitement right on the beach in Şile, Istanbul. We offer a unique experience for adults aged 18 to 45, whether you are an individual, a couple, friends, or a group. Get ready for an unforgettable adventure where relaxation meets activity, and memories are made to last a lifetime.
